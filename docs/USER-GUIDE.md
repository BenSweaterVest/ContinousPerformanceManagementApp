## For Supervisors

### Dashboard Overview

When you first open the app, you'll see the Dashboard with:

#### Key Metrics (4 Cards)
- **Total Staff**: Number of active staff you supervise
- **Evaluations This Month**: Completed evaluations count
- **Upcoming 1-on-1s**: Meetings scheduled in next 7 days
- **Pending Action Items**: Open tasks assigned to you

#### This Week's Suggestions

Yellow alert box shows:
- **Staff Member 1 + Question 1**: Suggested evaluation #1
- **Staff Member 2 + Question 2**: Suggested evaluation #2

These suggestions rotate weekly using a mathematical algorithm that ensures:
- Every staff member evaluated equally
- All 12 questions covered systematically
- Two evaluations per week

#### Recent Activity

Gallery showing your last 10 evaluations with:
- Staff name
- Question asked
- Rating given
- Date

### Weekly Evaluations

#### How to Complete an Evaluation

1. Click **Weekly Evaluations** in navigation menu

2. Review this week's suggestions (highlighted in yellow box)

3. For each suggested evaluation:
   - **Staff Member**: Pre-selected from suggestion (or choose different staff)
   - **Question**: Pre-selected from suggestion (or choose different question)
   - **Rating**: Click one of the rating buttons:
     - **1 - Not Satisfactory**: Performance significantly below expectations
     - **2 - Below Standard**: Performance needs improvement
     - **3 - Meets Standard**: Performance meets expectations
     - **4 - Exceeds Standard**: Performance above expectations
     - **5 - Exceptional**: Outstanding performance
     - **Insufficient Data**: Not enough observation this week
   - **Notes** (optional): Add specific examples or observations

4. Click **Save**

5. Evaluation recorded and appears in recent activity

#### When to Use "Insufficient Data"

Use this option when:
- Staff member was on vacation
- Limited interaction this week
- Not enough observation for fair rating
- New question area you haven't observed yet

**Note**: "Insufficient Data" still counts as a completed evaluation.

#### Ad Hoc Evaluations

Beyond the 2 weekly suggestions, you can complete additional evaluations:

1. Click **Add Additional Evaluation** section
2. Select any staff member
3. Select any question
4. Complete rating and notes
5. Save

**Type**: Automatically marked as "Ad Hoc"

### Managing Staff

#### Adding a New Staff Member

1. Click **Staff** in navigation menu
2. Click **+ Add Staff** button
3. Enter information:
   - **Name**: Full name (required)
   - **Employee ID**: Unique identifier
   - **Position Title**: Job title
   - **Start Date**: Position start date
   - **Supervisor**: Auto-set to you
   - **Status**: Select "Active"
4. Click **Save**

#### Viewing Staff Details

1. Click **Staff** in navigation
2. Click any staff member name
3. View tabs:
   - **Overview**: Recent evaluations, goals, meetings
   - **Performance History**: Charts and trends
   - **Development Plan**: IDP entries
   - **Recognition**: Positive feedback log

#### Editing Staff Information

1. Go to Staff Details screen
2. Click **Edit** button
3. Update fields
4. Click **Save**

#### Deactivating Staff

When staff leave or transfer:

1. Edit staff member record
2. Change **Status** to "Inactive"
3. Save

**Effect**: Staff removed from:
- Weekly rotation
- Dropdown lists
- Active dashboards

Historical data remains accessible.

### One-on-One Meetings

#### Preparing for a Meeting

1. Click **One-on-Ones** in navigation
2. Click **+ New Meeting**
3. Select **Staff Member**
4. Set **Meeting Date**
5. Enter **Agenda** (optional but recommended):
   - Recent achievements
   - Challenges/blockers
   - Development goals
   - Career discussion

#### During the Meeting

1. Open the staff member's detail page
2. Review:
   - Recent evaluation ratings
   - Performance trends
   - Current goals
   - Outstanding action items

3. Discuss and take notes

#### After the Meeting

1. Return to One-on-One screen
2. Complete the form:
   - **Discussion Notes**: Summary of conversation
   - **Action Items**: Follow-up tasks (list format)
3. Click **Save**

4. Optionally, copy action items to Action Items table for tracking

#### Meeting Notifications

You'll receive an email notification 15 minutes before meetings with supervised staff, including:
- Meeting details
- Link to staff performance data
- Recent evaluation summary

### Tracking Goals

#### Creating a Goal

1. Click **Goals** in navigation
2. Click **+ New Goal**
3. Enter:
   - **Staff Member**: Select staff
   - **Goal Description**: Specific, measurable objective
   - **Due Date**: Target completion
   - **Status**: Not Started
   - **Completion %**: 0%
4. Click **Save**

#### Updating Goal Progress

1. Find goal in list
2. Click to open
3. Update:
   - **Status**: Not Started → In Progress → Completed
   - **Completion %**: 0-100%
4. Save

### Recognition & Feedback

#### Logging Positive Recognition

1. Click **Recognition** in navigation
2. Click **+ New Recognition**
3. Enter:
   - **Staff Member**: Who to recognize
   - **Date**: When the achievement occurred
   - **Description**: What they did and why it matters
4. Click **Save**

**Best Practice**: Be specific and timely!
- "John solved the critical production issue within 2 hours, demonstrating excellent problem-solving under pressure"

Recognition entries:
- Visible to staff member
- Included in performance history
- Useful for annual reviews

### Action Items

#### Creating Action Items

1. Click **Action Items** in navigation
2. Click **+ New Action Item**
3. Enter:
   - **Description**: What needs to be done
   - **Owner**: Who is responsible
   - **Related Staff Member** (optional): If related to a specific person
   - **Due Date**: When it's due
   - **Status**: Pending
4. Click **Save**

#### Tracking Action Items

**Filter options:**
- **My Items**: Tasks assigned to you
- **All Items**: All tasks
- **Overdue**: Past due date

**Completing items:**
- Check the checkbox next to item
- Status automatically updates to "Completed"
- Completed date recorded

### Reports

#### Viewing Analytics

1. Click **Reports** in navigation
2. Select **Date Range**
3. View charts:
   - Average ratings by staff member
   - Evaluation completion rate
   - Goals completion status
   - Trends over time

#### Exporting Data

1. Click **Export** button
2. Select format (Excel)
3. Choose data:
   - All evaluations
   - Specific staff member
   - Date range
4. Download file

**Use cases:**
- Annual performance reviews
- Trend analysis
- Documentation for HR

## For Staff Members

### Dashboard

Your dashboard shows:
- Upcoming self-evaluation deadlines
- Recent feedback from supervisor
- Your goals and progress
- Action items assigned to you

### Quarterly Self-Evaluations

#### When to Complete

You'll complete self-evaluations **quarterly** in:
- **Q1 (Jul-Sep)**: Due early October
- **Q2 (Oct-Dec)**: Due early January
- **Q3 (Jan-Mar)**: Due early April
- **Q4 (Apr-Jun)**: Due early July

You'll receive email reminders at the start of each quarter.

#### How to Complete

1. Check email for reminder link
2. Click link to open app
3. Click **Self-Evaluation** in navigation
4. Answer all **12 questions**:
   - Rate yourself 1-5 for each
   - 1 = Not meeting expectations
   - 5 = Exceeding expectations
   - Be honest and reflective

5. Add **Notes** for each (recommended):
   - Examples of your work
   - Challenges you faced
   - Areas you're working to improve

6. Click **Save** for each question

7. Submit when all 12 are complete

#### Why Self-Evaluations Matter

- Compare your self-assessment with supervisor's ratings
- Identify areas of agreement/disagreement
- Guide development discussions
- Track your own growth over time

### Your Development Plan (IDP)

#### Viewing Your IDP

1. Click **My Development** in navigation
2. See your development goals:
   - Learning objectives
   - Skill development
   - Career growth targets

#### Adding IDP Entries

1. Click **+ New Goal**
2. Enter:
   - **Goal Description**: What you want to learn/achieve
   - **Target Date**: When you want to accomplish it
   - **Status**: Not Started
3. Click **Save**

#### Updating Progress

1. Click any IDP entry
2. Update:
   - **Status**: In Progress, Completed, On Hold
   - **Progress Notes**: What you've done, challenges, next steps
3. Save

**Examples of good IDP goals:**
- Complete Python certification course by June
- Shadow senior developer for code review process
- Present at team meeting on new technology

### Viewing Your Performance Data

#### Accessing Your History

1. Click **My Performance** in navigation
2. View:
   - **Recent Evaluations**: Supervisor's ratings
   - **Trends**: Performance over time
   - **Recognition**: Positive feedback received
   - **Goals**: Your objectives and progress

#### Understanding Your Ratings

Your supervisor rates you on 12 questions throughout the year:

1. Quality and accuracy in work products
2. Completing assignments on time
3. Effective communication
4. Proactive problem-solving
5. Technical competency
6. Adapting to change
7. Collaboration
8. Following processes
9. Customer service
10. Professional development
11. Workload management
12. Dependability

**Rotation system:**
- Each question rated about 4 times per year
- Comprehensive coverage across all areas
- Focuses on continuous micro-feedback vs. annual review shock

## Features Guide

### Rotation Algorithm

The system automatically suggests which staff and questions to evaluate each week.

**How it works:**

```
Week 1: Staff A, Question 1 + Staff B, Question 2
Week 2: Staff C, Question 3 + Staff D, Question 4
...continues rotating...
```

**Benefits:**
- Fair and equitable
- Comprehensive coverage
- Predictable schedule
- Removes bias from question selection

**You can override suggestions:**
- Evaluate any staff/question anytime
- Suggestions are guidance, not requirements

### Notifications

#### Email Notifications

You'll receive emails for:

**Supervisors:**
- Weekly evaluation reminders (Mondays, 8 AM)

**Staff:**
- Quarterly self-evaluation reminders

#### In-App Notifications

## Tips & Best Practices

### For Effective Evaluations

#### Be Specific
✅ "Completed database migration 2 days ahead of schedule with zero downtime"
❌ "Did good work"

#### Be Timely
- Evaluate during or shortly after observing performance
- Don't wait until end of week
- Fresh observations are more accurate

#### Be Balanced
- Rate the performance, not the person
- Use full scale (1-5)
- "Insufficient Data" is OK when appropriate

#### Use Notes Field
- Document specific examples
- Note context and circumstances
- Reference deliverables or tickets
- These notes are invaluable for annual reviews

### For Meaningful One-on-Ones

#### Consistency
- Schedule regular meetings (weekly or biweekly)
- Protect the time
- Cancel only when absolutely necessary

#### Two-Way Conversation
- Staff should talk more than supervisor
- Ask open-ended questions
- Listen actively

#### Forward-Looking
- Don't just review past performance
- Discuss upcoming challenges
- Plan development opportunities

#### Follow Up
- Track action items
- Review previous items
- Show progress matters

### For Development Planning

#### SMART Goals
- **S**pecific: Clear and precise
- **M**easurable: Track progress
- **A**chievable: Realistic
- **R**elevant: Aligned with role
- **T**ime-bound: Has a deadline

#### Regular Updates
- Review goals quarterly
- Update progress notes
- Adjust as priorities change

#### Link to Performance
- Connect IDP goals to evaluation questions
- Show how development improves performance
- Celebrate completed goals

## FAQs

### General

**Q: How do I log in?**
A: Use your Microsoft 365 account. The app uses your existing Teams/M365 credentials.

**Q: Can I use this on my phone?**
A: Yes, but tablet or desktop recommended for best experience.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes. Data stored in Microsoft Dataverse with enterprise security. Role-based access controls apply.

### For Supervisors

**Q: What if I don't complete the suggested evaluations?**
A: No problem! Suggestions are guidance. Complete evaluations when you have meaningful observations.

**Q: Can I evaluate the same staff/question multiple times?**
A: Yes. The system allows multiple evaluations to capture ongoing performance.

**Q: What if a staff member disagrees with a rating?**
A: Discuss during one-on-one. Rating reflects supervisor's assessment at that time. Can be discussed and clarified.

**Q: How do I handle staff on leave?**
A: Mark them as "Inactive" temporarily. No evaluations suggested while inactive. Reactivate when they return.

**Q: Can I see other supervisors' evaluations?**
A: No. Security model prevents cross-supervisor visibility.

### For Staff

**Q: Will I see every evaluation immediately?**
A: Evaluations are visible to you when created. Discuss any questions with your supervisor.

**Q: What if I miss a quarterly self-evaluation?**
A: Complete it as soon as possible. Talk to your supervisor if significantly delayed.

**Q: Can I update my IDP goals?**
A: Yes! Your IDP is owned by you. Update anytime.

**Q: Who sees my self-evaluations?**
A: Your supervisor and system administrators. Used for performance discussions.

### Technical

**Q: The app won't load. What should I do?**
A:
1. Refresh browser
2. Clear cache
3. Try different browser
4. Contact IT support

**Q: I don't see any staff members. Why?**
A: Check with your system administrator. Staff records may need to be created.

**Q: How do I export my data?**
A: Use the Reports screen. Click Export and select format.

**Q: Can I customize the 12 questions?**
A: Contact your system administrator. Questions can be updated in the admin portal.
